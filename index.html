Processing Fee and Payment Structure

Understand the payment stages before you apply.

The application processing fee is paid in two stages. The counterpart fund is separate and applies to any loan category.

Application processing fee: £200

The total application processing fee is £200. It is paid in two stages.

  • Initial payment: £100 to start processing
  • After visa: Remaining £100 balance

What the £200 covers

The processing fee covers key application and travel support services.

  • Admission processing
  • Loan offer support
  • Visa processing support
  • POF support guidance
  • Arrival and accommodation guide

Important loan note

For any loan category, a counterpart fund of £805 is required. Once the loan is secured, the loan is disbursed to the school within 24 hours. The £805 counterpart fund will be deducted from the amount the applicant is expected to pay back in the UK.

Important Information

Key things to know before you apply.

Read this section before submitting your details. It helps you understand the offer clearly and prevents unnecessary delays.

Who can apply?

Eligible Masters applicants with BSc, minimum Second Class Lower, CV, transcript, passport, and academic documents.

What does the funding cover?

The funding covers tuition only. Visa, living expenses, insurance, medicals, and travel costs are separate.

How is the processing fee paid?

The application processing fee is £200 total: £100 to start processing and £100 balance after visa.

What is the £805 counterpart fund?

It applies to any loan category and will be deducted from the amount the applicant is expected to pay back in the UK.

When is the loan disbursed?

Once the loan is secured, it is disbursed to the school within 24 hours.

Is undergraduate admission available?

Yes. Undergraduate admission is available, but there is no loan offer for undergraduate applicants.
